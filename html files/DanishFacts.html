Fun Facts About Denmark

Good Things to Know

  1. Denmark is a country located in Northern Europe. It is the southernmost of the Scandinavian countries and is bordered by Germany to the south and shares maritime borders with Sweden and Norway.

  2. The capital of Denmark is Copenhagen, which is also the largest city in the country. It is known for its beautiful architecture, historic sites, and bike-friendly culture.

  3. The official language of Denmark is Danish, and it is spoken by the majority of the population. English is also widely spoken and used in many aspects of daily life, making Denmark a highly English-friendly country.

  4. Denmark has a comprehensive welfare state with a universal healthcare system and free education, including higher education, for its citizens. It has a high standard of living and is often ranked highly in quality of life and happiness indexes.

  5. Denmark has a rich cultural heritage with famous literary figures like Hans Christian Andersen and influential design movements like Danish Modern. Danish cuisine is known for its open-faced sandwiches (smørrebrød), pastries (wienerbrød), and traditional dishes like pickled herring and frikadeller (meatballs).
