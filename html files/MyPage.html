Smørrebrød

  1. Smørrebrød is a traditional Danish open-faced sandwich.
  2. It typically consists of a slice of rye bread topped with various ingredients such as:
    • Pickled herring
    • Cold cuts
    • Cheese
    • Vegetables
  3. Smørrebrød is often enjoyed as a light meal or a snack in Denmark.
